# Unified Inbox

The **Inbox** brings every comment, direct message, review, and mention from all of your connected social platforms into one place. Instead of switching between Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, Bluesky, and Threads, you can read, reply, assign, and organize everything from a single screen.

<!-- screenshot: The Inbox page showing the three-column layout -- conversation list on the left, message thread in the center, and contact detail panel on the right -->

---

## Getting there

Click **Inbox** in the left sidebar. The page opens to the **Conversations** view with all conversations listed on the left.

---

## Layout

The Inbox uses a three-panel layout:

| Panel | What it shows |
|-------|---------------|
| **Left -- Conversation list** | All conversations, filtered and sorted. Each row shows the contact name, platform icon, a message preview, timestamp, and sentiment indicator. |
| **Center -- Message thread** | The full conversation history for the selected conversation, plus the reply composer at the bottom. |
| **Right -- Details drawer** | Contact information, labels, assignment, and the original social post. Toggle this panel with the **Details** button. |

---

## Viewing conversations

### Platform filter bar

At the top of the conversation list, platform icons let you filter by network. Click a platform icon to show only conversations from that platform. Click **ALL** to return to the full list.

<!-- screenshot: The platform icon bar showing ALL selected, with counts next to each platform icon -->

### Type tabs

Below the platform bar, four tabs split conversations by type:

- **All** -- every conversation
- **DMs** -- direct messages
- **Comments** -- replies and comments on your posts
- **Reviews** -- reviews from platforms that support them (e.g., Facebook)

Each tab shows a count badge so you can see the volume at a glance.

### Search

Type in the **Search conversations** field to filter the list by contact name, username, or message content. Results update as you type.

### Filters

The filter bar across the top of the page lets you narrow conversations by:

| Filter | Options |
|--------|---------|
| **Status** | Open, Pending, Resolved, Closed |
| **Platform** | Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, Bluesky, Threads |
| **Type** | Comments, DMs, Reviews, Mentions |
| **Sentiment** | Positive, Neutral, Negative |
| **Priority** | Urgent, High, Normal, Low |
| **Category** | Editorial, Business, Support, Community, Noise |
| **Date** | Today, Last 7 days, Last 30 days, Last 90 days |

Click a filter button to open its dropdown, select a value, and the list updates immediately. Click the same value again (or choose "All") to clear the filter. Active filters are highlighted.

### "Mine" toggle

Click the **Mine** button in the filter bar to show only conversations assigned to you. A badge shows how many are in your queue.

## Reading and replying to messages

Click a conversation in the left panel to open it in the center panel.

### Message thread

Messages appear as chat bubbles in chronological order. Each bubble shows the sender name, timestamp, and platform origin. Internal notes (visible only to your team) appear with a distinct background color.

### Replying

Type your reply in the **composer** at the bottom of the thread panel and press **Send** (or hit Enter). The reply is posted back to the original platform -- if it was an Instagram comment, your reply appears as a reply on Instagram.

**Replying to a specific message:** Click a message in the thread to set it as the reply target. A preview of the original message appears above the composer. Click the **X** to cancel and reply to the conversation generally.

**Attaching media:** Click the **paperclip** icon in the composer to add an image URL to your reply.

<!-- screenshot: The message composer with a reply-to preview and the send button -->

### Internal notes

Click the **sticky note** icon in the composer to switch to **Note mode**. Notes are visible only to your team -- they are never posted to the social platform. Use them to leave context for a colleague before handing off a conversation.

### @mention teammates

Type **@** in the composer to trigger a mention autocomplete. Select a teammate to tag them. Mentioned teammates receive a notification.

### Translation

If the conversation is in a different language, a **translate** button (globe icon) appears in the composer. Click it to translate your draft into the conversation's detected language before sending.

## Managing conversations

### Changing status

Open a conversation and use the **status controls** in the conversation header to move it through your workflow:

- **Open** -- needs attention
- **Pending** -- waiting for a response from the contact
- **Resolved** -- issue addressed
- **Closed** -- no further action needed

### Assigning to a team member

In the conversation header or the **Contact** detail panel, use the **assignee picker** to assign the conversation to a specific team member. The assignee sees it highlighted in their **Mine** queue.

### Snoozing

Click **Snooze** in the header to temporarily hide the conversation. It will reappear as open after the snooze period.

### Labels

Add labels to categorize conversations (e.g., "VIP", "Press", "Bug Report"). In the detail panel, click **Add Label** to attach one or remove it by clicking the **X** on an existing label.

### Flagging

Click the **star** icon on any conversation in the list to flag it for follow-up. Flagged conversations are visually highlighted.

## My Queue view

Switch from **Conversations** to **My Queue** using the toggle in the top bar. The queue view groups conversations into three buckets:

- **Assigned to Me** -- conversations assigned to you
- **Unassigned** -- conversations no one has claimed yet
- **All Team** -- every conversation with assignment info

This view is useful for picking up the next unassigned item or reviewing your team's workload.

<!-- screenshot: The My Queue view with the Unassigned tab selected -->

## Bulk actions

Select multiple conversations using the checkboxes on the left side of each conversation row. A **Bulk Action Bar** appears at the bottom with options to:

- **Mark as read**
- **Change status** (Open, Pending, Resolved, Closed)
- **Assign** to a team member
- **Add a label**

Click **Clear** to deselect all.

---

## Canned replies

Canned replies are pre-written responses you can insert with one click.

1. While composing a reply, type **/** (slash) to open the **Canned Reply Picker**.
2. Search by title, shortcut, or content.
3. Click a reply to insert it into the composer.

Canned replies automatically substitute the contact's name if it includes a placeholder. They can also be filtered by platform so you only see replies relevant to the current conversation.

## Real-time sync

A green **Syncing** indicator in the top bar confirms that the inbox is connected and receiving updates in real time. New messages appear in the conversation list without refreshing the page.

---

## Presence detection

When another team member is viewing the same conversation, a **Presence Banner** appears at the top of the thread showing their name. This prevents two people from replying to the same message at the same time.

---

## Crisis alerts

If the system detects a sudden spike in negative messages, a **Crisis Alert Banner** appears at the top of the inbox. The banner shows the severity (Warning or Critical), a summary, and the topics involved.

- Click **Resolve** to mark the crisis as handled.
- Click the **X** to dismiss the alert.

## Tips

- **Use the Mine toggle** to focus on your assigned conversations and avoid duplicating effort with teammates.
- **Set up canned replies** for your most common responses -- it saves time and keeps messaging consistent.
- **Add labels** to track conversation themes over time (e.g., "Product Feedback", "Sales Lead").
- **Use internal notes** to hand off context when assigning a conversation to someone else.
- **Check the crisis banner** at the start of your shift to catch any overnight issues.

## FAQ

**Are my replies posted directly to the social platform?**
Yes. When you reply to an Instagram comment, the reply appears on Instagram. When you reply to a Twitter/X DM, it goes back as a DM. The reply is posted through your connected account.

**Can I delete a message from the inbox?**
The inbox shows messages synced from social platforms. You can change the conversation status to Closed to archive it, but the original message on the social platform is not affected.

**What does the Sentiment badge mean?**
Social Suite's AI automatically analyzes the tone of each conversation and marks it as Positive, Neutral, or Negative. This helps you prioritize responses -- negative conversations may need faster attention.

**Can I filter by multiple values at once?**
Each filter dropdown supports one active value at a time, but you can combine different filters (e.g., Status = Open AND Sentiment = Negative AND Platform = Instagram).

**What platforms are supported?**
The inbox supports Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, Bluesky, and Threads.

**Do internal notes show up on social media?**
No. Internal notes are private to your team and never posted externally.
