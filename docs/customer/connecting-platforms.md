# Connecting Your Social Media Platforms

Get all your social accounts into one place so you can create, schedule, and publish content across every channel.

## Supported Platforms

Social Suite supports the following platforms:

| Platform | Page/Account Selection Required |
|----------|-------------------------------|
| Instagram | Yes -- you choose which Instagram account |
| Twitter / X | No -- connects directly |
| TikTok | No -- connects directly |
| LinkedIn | Yes -- you choose which LinkedIn Page |
| Facebook | Yes -- you choose which Facebook Page |
| YouTube | Yes -- you choose which YouTube Channel |
| Pinterest | No -- connects directly |
| Reddit | No -- connects directly |
| Bluesky | No -- connects directly |
| Threads | No -- connects directly |

---

## How to Connect a Platform

1. Open **Connections** from the left sidebar.
2. Find the platform you want to connect in the **Available Platforms** grid.
3. Click **Connect** on the platform card.
4. A new window opens asking you to sign in to that platform and grant Social Suite permission. No passwords are stored -- the connection uses secure OAuth tokens.
5. After you approve access, the window closes automatically.

<!-- screenshot: The Connections page showing the Available Platforms grid with Connect buttons -->

### Platforms That Require Page Selection

For **Instagram**, **LinkedIn**, **Facebook**, and **YouTube**, an extra step appears after you approve access:

1. A **Select Page** dialog opens listing every page, account, or channel you manage on that platform.
2. Click the one you want to connect.
3. Click **Connect**.

<!-- screenshot: The Page Selection dialog showing a list of Facebook Pages with avatars and a Connect button -->

If the list is empty, make sure you have admin access to at least one page or account on that platform.

## Viewing Connected Accounts

Once connected, each platform card shows:

- A **Connected** status indicator
- Your **username** on that platform
- Your **follower count** (formatted as 1.2K, 3.5M, etc.)

A summary bar at the bottom of the grid shows how many accounts you have connected in total.

Below the platform grid, the **Metrics by Platform** section shows performance data for every connected platform at a glance.

## Refreshing Connection Data

Click the **Refresh** button in the top-right corner of the Connections page to pull the latest follower counts and connection statuses from each platform.

---

## Disconnecting an Account

1. Open **Connections**.
2. Find the connected platform card.
3. Click **Disconnect**.
4. The account is removed immediately. You can reconnect it at any time by clicking **Connect** again.

Disconnecting does not delete any posts you have already published through Social Suite.

---

## Tips

- **Connect all the platforms you plan to publish to before composing your first post.** The compose wizard only shows platforms that are already connected.
- **One account per platform.** Each platform card connects a single account or page. If you need to manage multiple Facebook Pages, contact your admin about setting up additional companies.
- **Connections are company-scoped.** If you switch to a different company in the company switcher, you will see a different set of connections.

## FAQ

**Q: Do you store my password?**
No. Social Suite uses OAuth, which means you sign in directly on the platform's website. We receive a secure token -- never your password.

**Q: My connection stopped working. What should I do?**
Platform tokens can expire if you change your password or revoke access on the platform side. Disconnect the account in Social Suite and reconnect it to get a fresh token.

**Q: I approved access but the page selection dialog is empty.**
Make sure you have admin or manager access to at least one page/account on that platform. For Facebook, you need to be an admin of the Facebook Page. For Instagram, your account must be a Professional (Business or Creator) account linked to a Facebook Page.

**Q: Can I connect the same platform account to multiple companies?**
Yes. Each company maintains its own set of connections, so the same Instagram account can be connected to more than one company.

**Q: What permissions does Social Suite request?**
Only the minimum needed to read your profile information, publish posts on your behalf, and retrieve basic analytics. We never post without your explicit action (or an automation rule you set up).
