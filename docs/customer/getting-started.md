# Getting Started with Social Suite

Social Suite by Longtale.ai is a social media management platform built for media companies and publishing teams. It lets you connect all your social accounts in one place, generate AI-powered posts from your content feeds, schedule and approve posts across platforms, and track performance with built-in analytics.

---

## Creating Your Account

Follow these steps to create your Social Suite account:

1. Go to the Social Suite signup page and you will see the **Create an account** form.

2. Fill in the following fields:
   - **Full Name** — your first and last name as you want teammates to see it
   - **Email** — the email address you will use to sign in
   - **Password** — must be at least 6 characters
   - **Confirm Password** — re-enter your password to make sure it matches

3. Click the **Create Account** button.

4. Check your email inbox for a confirmation link. Click the link to verify your account and activate it.

5. Once confirmed, return to Social Suite and sign in.

<!-- screenshot: The signup form showing Full Name, Email, Password, and Confirm Password fields with the Create Account button -->

> **Joining via invitation?** If a teammate invited you, your email will be pre-filled and you will see a message saying "You've been invited to join your team!" Simply complete the signup form and you will be taken straight into the onboarding wizard.

### Forgot Your Password?

On the sign-in page, click **Forgot your password?**, enter your email, and click **Send Reset Link**. You will receive an email with a link to set a new password.

## Setting Up Your Company

After signing in for the first time, you will be taken to the **company setup** screen. Every Social Suite account belongs to a company — this is the workspace where your team collaborates and manages social media.

### If you have a pending invitation

If someone on your team already created a company and invited you, you will see your **Pending Invitations** listed at the top. Each invitation shows:

- The **company name**
- Your **role** (Owner, Admin, or Member)

Click the **Accept** button next to the invitation to join that company. You will be taken directly to your dashboard.

<!-- screenshot: The setup screen showing a pending invitation card with the company name, role badge, and Accept button -->

### If you are creating a new company

If you have no invitations (or prefer to start fresh), you will see the **Create Your Company** form:

1. Enter your **Company Name** — for example, "Acme Publishing."

2. The **URL Slug** will be generated automatically from your company name. You can customize it if you like. It can only contain lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens.

3. Click **Create Company**.

You will be taken to the onboarding wizard to finish setting up.

<!-- screenshot: The Create Your Company form showing the Company Name field, the auto-generated URL Slug, and the Create Company button -->

> **Tip:** If you already belong to a company and want to create an additional one later, you can do so from the company switcher in the sidebar.

## The Onboarding Wizard

After creating your company, Social Suite walks you through a three-step setup wizard. A progress bar at the top tracks where you are. You can skip any step and come back to it later.

<!-- screenshot: The onboarding wizard top bar showing the three numbered steps (Connect Accounts, Brand Voice, Setup Automation) with the progress bar -->

### Step 1: Connect Your Social Accounts

This step lets you link the social media platforms where you want to publish content. You will see cards for each supported platform:

- **Instagram**
- **Twitter / X**
- **LinkedIn**
- **Facebook**
- **TikTok**
- **YouTube**
- **Bluesky**
- **Threads**

Click **Connect** on any platform to start the secure OAuth process. A popup window will open where you sign in to that platform and grant Social Suite permission. No passwords are stored — the connection uses secure tokens.

For platforms like Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, and YouTube, you may be asked to **select a specific page or account** after authorizing.

> **Don't have access to the social accounts?** Click the **Invite** button to send a colleague an invitation. They can join your company and connect the accounts they manage.

Click **Continue** to move to the next step, or **Skip for now** if you want to connect accounts later.

<!-- screenshot: The Connect Your Social Accounts step showing the grid of platform cards with Connect buttons -->

### Step 2: Brand Voice

This step configures how Social Suite's AI generates content for your brand. You can customize:

- **Tone / Persona** — choose from four styles:
  - **The Anchor** — professional and balanced
  - **The Neighbor** — warm and approachable
  - **The Town Crier** — urgent and attention-grabbing
  - **The Instigator** — conversation starter

- **Content Length** — choose between Headline, Bullets, Standard, or Full Post

- **Emoji Style** — None, Smart (contextual), Minimal, or Heavy

- **Hashtags** — choose a strategy:
  - None
  - Smart Tags (AI-generated)
  - Brand Tags Only (your custom hashtags)
  - Smart + Brand Tags (both)

- **Custom Instructions** — optional free-text guidance for the AI, such as "Always mention our website" or "Avoid industry jargon."

Click **Save Voice Settings** to save your preferences, then click **Continue**.

The defaults work well out of the box, so feel free to **Skip for now** and refine your brand voice later from **Settings > Brand Voice**.

<!-- screenshot: The Brand Voice step showing the tone selector cards, content length toggle, and emoji style options -->

### Step 3: Setup Automation

This step creates your first automation rule. Automations watch your RSS feeds for new content and automatically generate social media posts from them.

Click **Create Automation Rule** to open the rule builder. The wizard will pre-fill sensible defaults:

- Your first RSS feed as the content source
- All connected social accounts as destinations
- Your email for approval notifications
- Posts are sent for your approval before publishing (nothing goes live without your say-so)

Click **Save** to activate the rule, then click **Finish Setup** to complete the wizard and go to your dashboard.

<!-- screenshot: The Setup Automation step with the Create Automation Rule button and the confirmation message showing the rule is active -->

## Connecting Your First Platform

You can connect (or disconnect) social media accounts at any time from the **Connections** page.

1. Click **Connections** in the left sidebar.
2. You will see cards for all supported platforms: Instagram, Twitter / X, TikTok, LinkedIn, Facebook, YouTube, Pinterest, Reddit, Bluesky, and Threads.
3. Click **Connect** on any platform to start the secure authorization flow.
4. Once connected, the card will show your username and follower count.
5. To disconnect, click the options menu on a connected card and choose **Disconnect**.

All connections use OAuth, meaning Social Suite never sees or stores your social media passwords. You can revoke access at any time.

## Your Dashboard

After completing setup, you land on your **Dashboard** — the home base for your Social Suite experience.

Here is what you will find:

- **Key Metrics** — at-a-glance stats for followers, impressions, engagement, and published posts, each with sparkline trend charts
- **AI Briefing** — a daily summary of your social media performance and suggested actions
- **Top Posts** — your best-performing recent posts across all platforms
- **Engagement Chart** — a visual breakdown of engagement over time
- **Upcoming Posts** — posts that are scheduled or awaiting approval
- **Inbox Summary** — a snapshot of your social media inbox activity
- **Platform Health** — connection status of all your linked accounts
- **Onboarding Progress** — a checklist showing which setup steps you have completed (disappears once you finish everything)

Use the **date range filter** in the top right to adjust the time period for your metrics.

The left sidebar gives you access to all major sections:

- **Content** — create, schedule, and manage posts; view your content calendar; manage drafts
- **Analytics** — detailed performance data and charts
- **Connections** — manage your connected social accounts
- **Settings** — update your profile, company details, brand voice, and notification preferences

## Tips

- **Start with one or two platforms.** You can always add more later from the Connections page. Getting familiar with the workflow on one platform first makes everything easier.

- **Set up your brand voice early.** Even a quick pass through the tone and emoji settings helps the AI generate content that feels like your brand from day one.

- **Invite your team.** Click the **Invite colleague** button during onboarding (or go to **Settings > Company** later) to add teammates. You can assign them roles like Admin or Member to control what they can access.

- **Nothing publishes without your approval.** The default automation sends posts for approval before publishing. You will receive an email with a one-click approve/reject link — no need to log in.

- **Use the content calendar.** Go to **Content > Calendar** to see all your scheduled and draft posts on a visual timeline. Drag and drop to reschedule.

- **Check the dashboard daily.** The AI Briefing and Top Posts sections give you a quick read on what is working and what needs attention.

## FAQ

**Q: How many social accounts can I connect?**
A: You can connect one account per platform. Social Suite supports Instagram, Twitter / X, TikTok, LinkedIn, Facebook, YouTube, Pinterest, Reddit, Bluesky, and Threads.

**Q: Is it safe to connect my social accounts?**
A: Yes. Social Suite uses OAuth, the industry-standard authorization protocol. Your social media passwords are never shared with or stored by Social Suite. You can revoke access at any time from the Connections page or from the social platform itself.

**Q: Can I invite teammates to my company?**
A: Yes. You can invite colleagues during onboarding or at any time from **Settings > Company**. Each person you invite receives an email with a signup link. You choose their role: Owner, Admin, or Member.

**Q: What happens if I skip a step in the onboarding wizard?**
A: Nothing breaks. You can come back and complete any step later. Connect accounts from the **Connections** page, configure your brand voice from **Settings > Brand Voice**, and set up automations from **Content > Automations**.

**Q: How do automations work?**
A: Automations monitor your RSS feeds for new articles or content. When something new is published, Social Suite's AI generates a social media post based on your brand voice settings. The post is then sent to you for approval via email. Once you approve it, it is published to your connected accounts.

**Q: Can I switch between multiple companies?**
A: Yes. If you belong to more than one company, use the **company switcher** in the sidebar to switch between them. Your selected company is remembered across sessions.

**Q: I did not receive the confirmation email. What should I do?**
A: Check your spam or junk folder. If you still cannot find it, return to the sign-in page and try signing in — if your account was auto-confirmed, you may already have access. Otherwise, try signing up again with the same email.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: On the sign-in page, click **Forgot your password?**, enter your email address, and click **Send Reset Link**. Follow the link in the email to set a new password.
