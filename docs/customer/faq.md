# Frequently Asked Questions

This FAQ covers common questions across all areas of Social Suite. For topic-specific questions, also check the FAQ sections in the [Getting Started](getting-started.md), [Connecting Platforms](connecting-platforms.md), [Collaboration](collaboration.md), [Settings](settings.md), and [Media Company](media-company.md) guides.

---

## Account and Access

**Q: Can I change the email address on my account?**
A: The email address on the Profile tab is read-only. Contact support to update it.

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Go to **Settings > Profile**, scroll to the **Security** section, and enter your new password in both fields. Click **Update Password**.

**Q: What are the different team roles?**
A: Social Suite has three roles:
- **Owner** -- full access to everything, including company deletion and billing.
- **Admin** -- can manage settings, approve/reject content, and invite members.
- **Member** -- can create content, use the inbox, and view analytics, but cannot approve posts or change company settings.

**Q: Can I belong to more than one company?**
A: Yes. Use the **company switcher** in the sidebar to move between companies. Your selected company is remembered across sessions.

## Platform Connections

**Q: My connection shows "disconnected" or "expired." What should I do?**
A: Go to **Connections**, find the platform, and click **Reconnect**. Some platforms (especially Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn) expire their tokens periodically. Reconnecting refreshes the token.

**Q: I connected my account but it is showing the wrong page/profile. How do I fix this?**
A: Disconnect the platform from the **Connections** page, then reconnect it. During the reconnection flow, you will be asked to select which page or account to link. Choose the correct one.

**Q: Does Social Suite store my social media passwords?**
A: No. All connections use OAuth, the industry-standard authorization protocol. Social Suite receives a secure token to post on your behalf but never sees your password. You can revoke access at any time from the Connections page or from the social platform itself.

**Q: Why can I not connect Pinterest or Reddit?**
A: Pinterest and Reddit are supported platforms, but their OAuth flows may have additional requirements. Make sure you have an active account on those platforms and that pop-ups are not blocked in your browser during the connection process.

## Content and Publishing

**Q: How do automations generate posts?**
A: Automations monitor your RSS feeds for new articles. When a new article appears, Social Suite's AI reads it and generates a social media post using your brand voice settings. The post is then sent for approval (via email or in-app notification) before it goes live.

**Q: Can I edit a post after it has been generated by AI?**
A: Yes. AI-generated posts appear as drafts. You can edit the text, change the image, add or remove platforms, and adjust anything before approving and scheduling.

**Q: What is the difference between Drafts and Scheduled posts?**
A: **Drafts** are posts that are being worked on but not yet queued for publishing. **Scheduled** posts have been approved and assigned a specific date and time to go live.

**Q: Can I schedule a post for a specific date and time?**
A: Yes. After a post is approved, you can schedule it by picking a date and time. You can also drag posts on the **Content > Calendar** view to reschedule them.

**Q: What happens if I pull a published post?**
A: Pulling a post changes its status to **Pulled** in Social Suite for tracking purposes. It does not delete the post from the social platform -- you would need to do that directly on the platform.

**Q: Can I post to multiple platforms at once?**
A: Yes. When creating a post, select all the platforms you want to publish to. The AI generates platform-specific versions (adjusting length, hashtags, and formatting for each platform).

## Analytics

**Q: Where do I see my analytics?**
A: Click **Analytics** in the left sidebar. You will see charts and metrics for engagement, followers, impressions, and top-performing posts across all your connected platforms.

**Q: How often is analytics data updated?**
A: Analytics data is synced periodically. Recent posts may take a few hours to show updated engagement numbers. The dashboard shows the most recent data available.

**Q: Can I filter analytics by platform?**
A: Yes. Use the platform filter on the Analytics page to view data for a specific platform or compare across platforms.

**Q: What does the "engagement rate" metric mean?**
A: Engagement rate is the ratio of interactions (likes, comments, shares, saves) to total reach or impressions. A higher rate means your audience is actively responding to your content.

## Inbox

**Q: What is the Inbox?**
A: The Inbox is a unified view of all your social media interactions -- comments, direct messages, reviews, and mentions -- across all connected platforms. It lets you respond to everything from one place.

**Q: Can I reply to comments and DMs from within Social Suite?**
A: Yes. Open a conversation in the Inbox and type your reply in the message composer. For comments, your reply will be posted publicly on the platform. For DMs, it is sent as a private message.

**Q: What are internal notes?**
A: Internal notes are messages visible only to your team. Use them to discuss a conversation without the contact seeing it. Click the sticky note icon in the message composer to switch to note mode.

**Q: Can I assign an inbox conversation to a teammate?**
A: Yes. Assigning conversations works similarly to assigning drafts. The assigned person receives a notification and the conversation appears in their assignment queue.

## Brand Voice and AI

**Q: What is Brand Voice?**
A: Brand Voice is a set of rules that tell the AI how to write social media posts for your brand. It includes tone, content length, emoji preferences, hashtag strategy, and custom instructions.

**Q: How do I test my brand voice settings before saving?**
A: Use the **Content Playground** at the bottom of the Brand Voice settings page. Select an article from your feeds and click **Generate** to see a sample post using your current settings.

**Q: What does "AI Decides" mode do?**
A: In AI Decides mode, the AI has full autonomy to pick the best tone, length, emoji style, and hashtags for each article. It ignores your custom settings and optimizes for engagement. Enable **Require review before publishing** if you want to check the results before they go live.

**Q: I set up my brand voice but posts still sound generic. What should I do?**
A: Try adding **Custom Instructions** with specific guidance. For example: "Use a conversational tone, always ask a question at the end, and reference our tagline 'News That Matters.'" The more specific your instructions, the more personalized the output.

## OG Images

**Q: What are OG images?**
A: OG (Open Graph) images are the preview images that appear when someone shares a link on social media. Social Suite can automatically generate branded OG images for your posts using your logo, colors, and one of the available templates.

**Q: Can I control which templates the AI uses?**
A: Yes. Go to **Settings > OG Images > Template Library**. Click any template to enable or disable it. Disabled templates will not be selected by the AI.

## Troubleshooting

**Q: The page is showing a white screen or error overlay. What should I do?**
A: Try refreshing the page. If the error persists, clear your browser cache and cookies, then log in again. If the problem continues, contact support with a screenshot of the error.

**Q: I am not receiving notification emails. What should I check?**
A: First, verify that email notifications are enabled in **Settings > Notifications** for the relevant event types. Then check your spam or junk folder. If notifications are enabled and not in spam, contact support.

**Q: My RSS feed is not picking up new articles. Why?**
A: Make sure the RSS feed URL is correct and publicly accessible. Some feeds require authentication or have rate limits. Go to **Content > Feeds** to verify the feed status and check for error messages.

**Q: Posts are stuck in "Awaiting Approval" and I cannot approve them. What is wrong?**
A: Only Owners and Admins can approve posts. If you are a Member, ask an Owner or Admin on your team to review the post. If you are an Owner or Admin and still cannot approve, try refreshing the page or logging out and back in.
