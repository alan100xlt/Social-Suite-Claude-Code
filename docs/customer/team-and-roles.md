# Team Members and Roles

Social Suite supports collaborative team workflows with role-based access control. Every person on your team gets the right level of access -- from full administrative control to day-to-day content management.

<!-- screenshot: The Team Members section on the Company settings page showing member cards with role badges (Owner, Admin, Member) -->

---

## Getting there

Go to **Settings** in the left sidebar, then click the **Company** tab. The **Team Members** section appears at the bottom of the page.

---

## The three roles

Every team member has one of three roles. The role determines what they can see and do within your company workspace.

### Owner

The person who created the company. There is one Owner per company.

| Capability | Owner |
|-----------|-------|
| View all content, analytics, and inbox | Yes |
| Create and schedule posts | Yes |
| Reply to inbox messages | Yes |
| Invite and remove team members | Yes |
| Change member roles | Yes |
| Manage RSS feeds and automation rules | Yes |
| Edit company name and branding | Yes |
| Manage social platform connections | Yes |
| Delete the company | Yes |

### Admin

Admins have broad access and can manage most aspects of the company, except they cannot delete the company itself.

| Capability | Admin |
|-----------|-------|
| View all content, analytics, and inbox | Yes |
| Create and schedule posts | Yes |
| Reply to inbox messages | Yes |
| Invite and remove team members | Yes |
| Manage RSS feeds and automation rules | Yes |
| Edit company name and branding | Yes |
| Manage social platform connections | Yes |
| Delete the company | No |

### Member

Members can work with content and respond in the inbox but cannot change company settings or manage the team.

| Capability | Member |
|-----------|--------|
| View all content, analytics, and inbox | Yes |
| Create and schedule posts | Yes |
| Reply to inbox messages | Yes |
| Invite and remove team members | No |
| Manage RSS feeds and automation rules | No |
| Edit company name and branding | No |
| Manage social platform connections | No |
| Delete the company | No |

## Inviting a team member

Only **Owners** and **Admins** can invite new members.

1. Go to **Settings > Company**.
2. In the **Team Members** section, click the **Invite** button.
3. In the dialog that opens:
   - Enter the person's **email address**.
   - Choose a **role**: **Member** or **Admin**.
4. Click **Send Invitation**.

The invitee receives an email with a link to sign up and join your company. Once they accept, they appear in the team list with their assigned role.

<!-- screenshot: The Invite Team Member dialog with an email field and a role dropdown showing Member and Admin options -->

### Pending invitations

After sending an invitation, it appears in the **Pending Invitations** section below the team list. From there, Owners and Admins can:

- **View** the invitation details
- **Copy** the invitation link to share manually
- **Revoke** a pending invitation if it was sent in error

Pending invitations expire after a set period. If the invitee has not accepted, you can revoke and re-send.

## Viewing team members

The **Team Members** section displays a card for each person on the team. Each card shows:

- **Avatar and name**
- **Email address**
- **Role badge** -- Owner (gold), Admin (blue), or Member (gray)

## Editing company information

Owners and Admins can update company details from the **Company** tab in **Settings**.

### Company name

1. In the **Company Information** card, click **Edit**.
2. Type the new name and click the **checkmark** to save, or **X** to cancel.

### Brand identity

The **Brand Identity** card displays your logo, brand colors, description, location, and website. To edit:

1. Click **Edit** on the Brand Identity card.
2. Update any of the fields:
   - **Logo** -- paste a URL to your logo image
   - **Brand Colors** -- enter hex color codes separated by commas
   - **Description** -- a short summary of your brand
   - **Location** -- your city or region
   - **Website** -- your company website URL
3. Click **Save Branding**.

## Media company association

If your company is part of a larger media organization, the **Media Company** card shows which parent organization you belong to and your relationship type. This association is managed by the media company admin and cannot be changed from the company settings page.

## Tips

- **Start with the right roles.** Give Admin access only to people who need to manage feeds, connections, and team members. Everyone else can work effectively as a Member.
- **Use clear names.** Ask team members to set their full name in their profile so the team list and inbox assignments are easy to read.
- **Revoke unused invitations.** If someone did not accept within a few days, revoke the old invitation and send a fresh one.
- **Review the team list periodically.** Remove members who have left the organization to keep your workspace secure.

## FAQ

**Can I change someone's role after they join?**
Role changes are managed through the company membership settings. Contact your Owner or Admin to adjust roles.

**Can there be more than one Owner?**
Each company has one Owner -- the person who originally created it. If you need to transfer ownership, contact support.

**What happens when I remove a team member?**
They lose access to the company workspace immediately. Their past actions (posts created, inbox replies) remain in the system for audit purposes, but they can no longer log in to see the company.

**Can a Member see analytics?**
Yes. All roles can view analytics, inbox, and content. The difference is that Members cannot change company settings, manage connections, or invite new team members.

**I sent an invitation but the person never received it.**
Check the **Pending Invitations** section to confirm the invitation was created. If the email did not arrive, the invitee should check their spam folder. You can also copy the invitation link directly and share it via another channel.

**Can I invite someone as an Owner?**
No. The invitation dialog offers only **Member** and **Admin** roles. Ownership is assigned to the company creator.

**Is there a limit to how many team members I can invite?**
There is no hard limit on team size. Invite as many people as your organization needs.
